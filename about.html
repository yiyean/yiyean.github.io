About Mystery Dessert Diary

Mystery Dessert Diary was founded in 2023 by Edvina Loh, a passionate bakers who wanted to share their love of desserts with the world. The mission is to provide you with the best dessert recipes, tips, and ideas so that you can create delicious treats in the comfort of your own home. Whether you're an experienced baker or just starting out, we have something for everyone. From classic cakes and pies to trendy new desserts, our collection has it all. We also provide step-by-step instructions, ingredient lists, and videos to help you perfect your creations. So why not join our community of dessert lovers and start baking today? We can't wait to see what you create!
